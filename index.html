📜 Quick Rules

4
4 vs 4 format — Size 3 ball. Minimum 3 players to start.
👕
Home = Blue, Away = White — Check the schedule for your jersey color each week!
🛑
No heading, no slide tackling — Safety first for our little ones!
⏰
Equal playing time — Every player plays at least half of each game.
⚽
No corner kicks — Ball over the goal line = goal kick.
⚖️
5+ goal lead? — Trailing team gets to add an extra player for balance.
🪑
Coaches ref — Coaches officiate the games (bring a whistle and stopwatch!).
🌟
Have fun! — This is PeeWee soccer. It's all about learning and having a blast!
Full rules at wpdathletics.org

⚠️ Safety & Weather

⚡

Lightning / Thunder

ALL play stops immediately. Everyone off the field. Wait 30 minutes after the last flash/boom before returning.

🌧️

Rain / Field Closures

Check RainoutLine for field status. We'll email updates by 3 PM on game/practice days.

👪

Pickup Policy

No child will be released without a parent or guardian present. Please be on time for pickup!
